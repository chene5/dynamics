Naturalistic Thinking Study

Debriefing


This study was designed to assess naturalistic thinking—the tacit, experiential, and intuitive reasoning of everyday interaction. We are investigating how naturalistic thinking can help us measure creativity and imagination.

Your participation will help us understand how we might be able to measure and improve individuals’ capacities for creativity and imagination.

Thank you for your participation in this study. We ask that you don’t share the hypotheses of this experiment with anyone else who might take part in this research.
